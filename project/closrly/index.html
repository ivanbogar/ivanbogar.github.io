Closrly: Visual Identity & App

Closrly was an independent project I developed for an investor in 2014.

The goal was to create a super application that added functionality similar to Foursquare, Facebook Groups, Olx, Tinder and Whatsapp.

Even without being completed and officially launched, it was a challenge that generated a lot of knowledge.
