Dow: Pest Guide

Dow AgroSciences, formerly a wholly owned subsidiary of the Dow Chemical Company, and recently acquired by Corteva Agriscience, specializes in products aimed at the agricultural industry, such as pesticides, seeds and biotechnology.

Challenges

In order to offer innovation and generate new opportunities to serve its stakeholders and customers in the agricultural production market regionally, Dow identified the possibility of creating a self-powered community through a collaborative application.

In this way, each user could document pests and plants through photos, with the help of the community to identify, classify and curate them.
