Rockout: Visual Identity

Digital Agency, Brazil

The name is inspired by the song "rock out" from Motörhead's album Motörizer, whose lyrics say, among other things, that rock will change YOUR life, will make you SHINE, that YOU can do anything, that it will IMPRESS the girls, etc.

And this is the philosophy that the company wants to convey in its work, with efficiency, originality and a hint of irreverence. With a modern and at the same time classic look.
