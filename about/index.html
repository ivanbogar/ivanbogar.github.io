About Ivan Bogar

More than 20 years working, learning and sharing experiences and design for interfaces, interactivity, information architecture, web and mobile prototyping, without forgetting to continue practicing my skills as a front-end developer with HTML / CSS, JavaScript, PHP and AngularJS.

Currently working as UX/UI Designer at IBM in Sao Paulo, I'm driven by the search for the best functional and intuitive experiences, balancing the best results for the business through user-centered design decisions.

I worked for different types of industries, such as Finance, Fintechs, E-larning, E-commerce, Energy, Services and in all of them with a strong mission behind the work - sharing knowledge, commitment and ethics.

I strongly believe that design and storytelling are powerful ways to create change.

I feel comfortable working on a variety of projects, from designing native apps to collaborating with the developer team. Whether editing videos, illustrating on paper or prototyping, I always seek to bring a quality of work and creativity that is difficult to match.

Outside of work, I really enjoy running, cooking, building things, and traveling with my wife and kids.
